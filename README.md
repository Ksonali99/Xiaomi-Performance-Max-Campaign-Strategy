# 📱 Xiaomi Google Performance Max Campaign Strategy

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on building a comprehensive Google Performance Max campaign strategy for Xiaomi to drive online sales, conversions, and full-funnel customer engagement across multiple Google inventory channels in India.

The campaign strategy leverages AI-powered automation, audience signals, smart bidding, and segmented asset groups to maximize conversion efficiency and improve campaign performance across Search, YouTube, Display, Gmail, and Discover.

## 🚀 Key Project Features

* Google Performance Max campaign setup
* Multi-channel Google Ads strategy
* AI-powered bidding and campaign optimization
* Audience signal and search theme planning
* Conversion-focused campaign structure
* Asset group segmentation strategy
* Product-focused asset group planning
* Smartphone and accessories targeting
* Search theme optimization
* Offer-based promotional campaign setup
* Multi-city geo-targeting strategy
* Performance Max creative strategy
* Full-funnel advertising approach
* Automated Google Ads optimization workflows

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The main objective of this project is to demonstrate how Google Performance Max campaigns use machine learning, audience signals, and automated placements to optimize conversions and improve advertising performance across Google’s ecosystem.

The strategy focuses on:

* Increasing online sales and conversions
* Expanding reach across multiple Google channels
* Improving audience targeting and campaign efficiency
* Enhancing customer engagement through personalized ads
* Driving performance through automated bidding and optimization
